To help visitors access all features of our site, we made an accessability function on our web site. To get access for any page just follow the instruction below:

  • Press "h" on keyboard to access Main Page
  • Press "a" on keyboard to access About Page
  • Press "c" on keyboard to access Contact Us Page
  • Press "b" on keyboard to access Accessability Page
  • Press "r" on keyboard to access Registration Page
  • Press "l" on keyboard to access Log In Page

Keep in mind, that you have to press additional button in pair with keys above to access pages with keyboard. It depends on your browser, so follow the link to find your additional button.
